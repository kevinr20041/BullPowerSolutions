Privacy notice

Last updated: 15 July 2026

This website belongs to Bull Power Solutions, an electrical contracting business based in Co. Kildare, Ireland. This page explains what happens to your data when you use this site. The short version: this site keeps almost nothing.

What this site does not do

This site sets no cookies, runs no analytics or tracking, has no user accounts, and has no database. Nothing you type into the quote form is sent to or stored on our servers.

The quote form and WhatsApp

When you fill in the quote form and press "Send job details", your device opens WhatsApp with a pre-written message containing the details you entered. Nothing is transmitted anywhere until you press send inside WhatsApp yourself. From that point the message is handled by WhatsApp, which is operated by Meta Platforms and covered by WhatsApp's own privacy policy.

Phone calls and messages

If you call, text or WhatsApp us, we use your contact details only to respond to your enquiry and arrange work. We do not add you to marketing lists or share your details with anyone else.

Hosting logs

This site is hosted by Vercel Inc. Like nearly all web hosts, Vercel keeps standard technical logs (including IP addresses) for security and operations. See Vercel's privacy policy.

Fonts

The site's fonts load from Google Fonts. When the page loads, your browser requests the font files from Google's servers, which means Google receives your IP address as part of that request. See Google's privacy policy.

Your rights

Under the GDPR you have rights over personal data we hold about you, including access, correction and erasure. Since this site stores nothing, in practice this applies to enquiry details you've sent us by phone or WhatsApp. To exercise any of these rights, contact Nathan on 085 127 5801 or Jack on 085 804 8772. You also have the right to complain to the Data Protection Commission, Ireland's supervisory authority.

Changes

If this site ever starts using cookies or analytics, this notice will be updated first and a consent banner added where required.
